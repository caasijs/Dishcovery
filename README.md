<b>OVERVIEW</b><br/>
Food waste remains a growing concern as many households struggle to maximize the use of available ingredients. Dishcovery addresses this problem by providing recipe recommendations based on ingredients users already have at home while promoting sustainable food practices through food scrap utilization tips and nutritional information.

<b>FEATURES</b><br/> <b>Ingredient-Based Recipe Suggestions</b><br/>
* Enter available ingredients<br/>
* Receive personalized recipe recommendations<br/>
* Reduce food waste by maximizing ingredient usage<br/>
